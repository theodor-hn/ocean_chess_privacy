Developer: TN Studios (Individual Developer)

2. Information We Collect

Ocean Chess is designed to minimize the collection of personal information.

We do NOT collect personal information such as:

  • Your name

  • Home address
  • GPS or precise location
  • Contacts

3. Analytics

Ocean Chess uses Unity Analytics to better understand how players interact with the Game and to improve gameplay, stability, and performance.

Unity Analytics may automatically collect information such as:

  • Gameplay events
  • Session length
  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • Country or region (derived from IP address by Unity)
  • Crash reports
  • Performance statistics
  • General gameplay behavior

This information is used to analyze and improve the Game. TN Studios does not use this information to identify individual users.

4. Advertising

Ocean Chess uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

Google AdMob may collect certain information necessary for:

  • Displaying advertisements
  • Measuring advertising performance
  • Preventing fraud
  • Limiting repeated advertisements
  • Providing personalized advertisements where permitted by applicable law

Depending on your region, Google may request your consent before serving personalized advertisements. For more information about Google's data practices, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

5. In-App Purchases

Ocean Chess offers optional in-app purchases. All purchases are securely processed through:

  • Google Play Billing (Android)
  • Apple App Store In-App Purchases (iOS)

TN Studios does not receive, process, or store your payment card information. Payment information is handled entirely by Google or Apple under their respective privacy policies.

6. Local Game Data

Ocean Chess stores game progress locally on your device using Unity PlayerPrefs. This may include:

  • Completed levels
  • Game settings
  • Player preferences
  • Game progress
  • Other gameplay-related information

This data remains stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to TN Studios. Deleting the application or clearing app data may permanently remove this information.

7. Third-Party Services

The Game uses the following third-party services:

These third-party providers operate independently from TN Studios. Their collection and processing of information is governed by their own privacy policies.
